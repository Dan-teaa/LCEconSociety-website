The Economic Insights Publication

Next week we will be publishing our September Issue of The Economic Insights. A huge thank you to all our writers, and our Publisher Kevin Jackson. We are all excited to read about the issues and events that our members take interest in.

For those of you who are interested in writing for the October issue, I encourage you to check out the Publication Writing Agenda. Find the October Agenda on Page 7, and fill in your name, email, and article topic. You should aim to have a rough draft completed for our Writing Workshop on October 17th, from 1:00pm - 2:00pm in SL11c.
